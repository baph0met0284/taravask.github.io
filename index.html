NOX Util Discord Bot

About

NOX Util is a versatile Discord bot designed to enhance the functionality and enjoyment of Discord servers. With a focus on moderation and music-related features, the bot aims to provide a seamless experience for server administrators and members alike.

Features

  • Moderation: NOX Util includes a set of moderation features to help maintain a positive and respectful environment within Discord servers. Administrators can utilize these tools to manage user behavior and content.
  • Music Playback: Enjoy music within your Discord server with NOX Util's music-related features. Play, pause, skip, and control the music playback experience in voice channels.
